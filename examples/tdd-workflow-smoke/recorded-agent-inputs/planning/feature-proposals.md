# Sprint candidate features

## F1-file-bug
**One-line:** File a new bug with title, description, and initial status.
**Rationale:** Step 1 of product arc. User-facing capability; foundation for retrieval and transition.
**E2E story required:** Yes. Form submission and stable bug ID generation.
**Priority:** P0

## F2-view-bug
**One-line:** Retrieve and display a bug by its stable ID.
**Rationale:** Step 1 of product arc. User-facing capability; enables re-finding and sharing bugs.
**E2E story required:** Yes. Navigation to bug detail view.
**Priority:** P0

## F3-transition-status
**One-line:** Update a bug's status to a valid state.
**Rationale:** Step 1 of product arc. User-facing capability; enables workflow progression. Supports NFR R2 (status validation).
**E2E story required:** Yes. Status change and visible confirmation.
**Priority:** P0
